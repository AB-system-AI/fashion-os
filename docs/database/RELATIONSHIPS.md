## Hub tables

- `tenants` — root tenant scope for most child tables via `tenant_id`.
- `stores` — operational scope for POS, sales, expenses, and store-scoped settings.
- `product_variants` — joins catalog to inventory, sales, purchases, and transfers.
- `sale_orders` — anchors payments, returns, coupons, and tax lines.
